Vision - Change and Progress

Dr. Anbumani is a Member of Parliament (Lok Sabha) from Dharmapuri in Tamil Nadu since 2014. Dharmapuri district is situated in the north western corner of Tamil Nadu and is bounded by Tiruvannamalai and Villupuram districts on the east, Salem district on the South, Krishnagiri district on the north and Kaveri River on the west. The district economy is mainly agrarian in nature. Nearly 70% of the workforce is dependent on agriculture and allied activities.
